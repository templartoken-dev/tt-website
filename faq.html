FAQ

What is the Knights Templar Token?

The Knights Templar Token (KT) is a CeDeFi (Centralized-Decentralized Finance) project built on the Binance Smart Chain. It aims to provide passive income and blockchain-based financial services while promoting integrity, honor, and respect in the banking sector.

How can I buy and HODL Knights Templar Tokens?

You can buy KT tokens on Pancake Swap after the public ICO launch. Simply connect your wallet to Pancake Swap, search for the KT token using its contract address, and swap your preferred currency for KT tokens. Remember to HODL your tokens to earn passive income and unlock additional features.

What are the tokenomics of Knights Templar Token?

Knights Templar Token has a total supply of 1,000,000,000,000 tokens. The tokenomics include a 10% transaction fee on every buy, sell, or send action. The fee is distributed as follows: 2% to liquidity pools, 2% to all holders as reflection income, 2% to the burn wallet, 2% for buybacks and liquidity, and 2% to the Developers Marketing & Charity Wallet.

How does the passive income system work?

KT token holders earn passive income through reflection income and staking. A 2% reflection fee is distributed among all holders for every transaction, including the burn wallet. By staking KT tokens, you can also unlock banking services, loans, and insurance.

What is the roadmap for Knights Templar Token?

The roadmap includes the following milestones: ICO launch, public contract, liquidity partnerships, branding and marketing, wallet app development, blockchain and exchange development, hard wallets, exchange launch, debit/credit card launch, and banking services such as crypto loans and insurance. The timeline for these milestones can be found on the Roadmap page.
