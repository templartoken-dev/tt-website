Welcome to Templar Token

Templar Token is a conceptual ERC-20 token based on the original Knight of Templar banking system. Our goal is to combine the rich history of the Knights Templar with the modern technology of blockchain.

Experience the nostalgia of times while taking advantage of the security and decentralization provided by the Ethereum network.
